Ewnetu Abebe

My research areas are Data-driven Predictive Modeling for Natural Disasters. Specifically, I study Earthquake magnitude prediction for the Horn of Africa using deep learning. Also, I study Machine Learning, Time Series Analysis:Investigating techniques for analyzing and predicting time-dependent data, such as stock prices, weather patterns, or sensor data. My research in this area involve exploring autoregressive models, recurrent neural networks, or anomaly detection algorithms.

My MSc. Thesis, defended in February 2023, was Earthquake magnitude prediction for the Horn of Africa using deep learning. My Supervisor was Hailemichael Kebede.
